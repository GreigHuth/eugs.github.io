Eclipse Esports - University of Edinburgh

Welcome to the webpage for the University of Edinburgh's esports teams! Our players compete in both the NUEL and NSE leagues. Join our Discord server if you want to chat or play alongside us.

Our Teams

We already field teams in a variety of popular competitive titles, and we're always happy to create new teams for more games.

Our CSGO team this year is large and we are looking to split it into multiple teams.

Both of our Valorant teams represent the univeristy well.

We are fielding two Siege teams this semester after the first team's success during the winter season.

Eclipse is hosting two Rocket League teams currently.
